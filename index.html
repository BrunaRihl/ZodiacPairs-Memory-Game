The constellations of the zodiac signs are groupings of stars that, when observed from Earth, form distinct patterns in the nighttime sky. They play a significant role in both astrology and popular culture, where each zodiac sign is associated with a specific constellation. These constellations provide a unique perspective on personality and human traits, making them a captivating part of both astrology and popular culture.

First, click on "start!" The timer will begin counting the game time.

Click on two cards to flip them. If they match, you create a pair, and they will stayflipped. If they don't match, they will flip back over.

The objective of the game is to find all pairs of identical cards in the fewest number of moves and the shortest possible time.

Victory:

Win by finding all the pairs.

Unmatched Pairs: -5 points!

Time Deduction: The seconds used during the game are subtracted from the final score.
